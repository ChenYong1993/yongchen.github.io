Experiences:

Since March 2021, I have been working at the School of Computer and Information Engineering, Jiangxi Normal University, Nanchang.

In December 2020, I received my Ph.D. degree from the School of Mathematical Science at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), advised by Prof. Ting-Zhu Huang and Prof. Xi-Le Zhao.

From October 2018 to September 2019, I was a Research Intern at Geoinformatics Unit, RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project, Japan, advised by Prof. Naoto Yokoya and Prof. Wei He.

My research interests are remote sensing image processing, computer vision, and machine learning.
