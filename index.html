Experiences:

I am now working at the School of Computer and Information Engineering, Jiangxi Normal University, Nanchang.

In 2020, I received my Ph.D. degree in the School of Mathematical Science at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), advised by Prof. Ting-Zhu Huang and Prof. Xi-Le Zhao.

From Oct. 2018 to Sept. 2019, I was a Research Intern with Geoinformatics Unit, RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project, Japan, advised by Dr. Naoto Yokoya and Dr. Wei He.

My research interests are remote sensing image processing, computer vision, and machine learning.
