Experiences:

I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in the School of Mathematical Science at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), advised by Prof. Ting-Zhu Huang (黄廷祝 教授).

From Oct. 2018 to Sept. 2019, I was a Research Intern with Geoinformatics Unit, RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project, Japan, advised by Dr. Naoto. Yokoya and Dr. Wei He.

My research interests are image processing, computer vision, and machine learning.
